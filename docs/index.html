Are you an indie developer?

Do you want to easily receive videos from your playtesters?

I'm interested

Playcocola is a playtesting platform specially made for indie developers. It is simple, easy to use and inexpensive.

Just send an invitation link to your playtesters that will allow them to easily record a video of their session and send it to you by just clicking a button.

Give them an appreciation reward for their effort. It can be anything from a couple of euros on a steam voucher to just a thank you message.

Visualize all the playtest sessions directly online or download them to your computer. Recontact your playtesters if you need it.
